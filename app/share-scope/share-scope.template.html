Sub Controllers Bindings

When nesting controllers, with controller as syntax; child controller can access parent controllers properties, but parent controller can not access child controllers properties.

Nesting directives

When nesting a directive under a controller, and the directive does not have an isolated scope, the controller can access its properties. And the directive can access its parent controllers properties.

Important

In controller functions, no strait forward way to reach properties of parent/child scope.
